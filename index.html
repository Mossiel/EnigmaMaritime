About Enigma Maritime

Founded in 2022

Established in the Dominican Republic, we have grown to become a leading provider of maritime inspection and laboratory services across Latin America and the Caribbean.

International Presence

With offices and laboratories across multiple countries, we provide comprehensive coverage and local expertise to serve our clients' needs throughout the region.

Our Services

Comprehensive maritime and laboratory services designed to meet the highest industry standards

Q&Q Inspection

Real-time cargo supervision and legal certification based on API, ASTM, and IFIA standards. Ensuring quantity and quality compliance for all maritime operations.

Laboratory Analysis

24/7 physical-chemical analysis of hydrocarbons, petrochemicals, agriculture, and minerals with advanced testing equipment and certified procedures.

Fiscal Control

Fuel quantity and quality verification, subsidy validation, and strategic statistical reporting for government and regulatory compliance.

Loss Control

Client representation with real-time reports and comprehensive terminal evaluations to minimize losses and maximize operational efficiency.

Inventory Control

Tank and pipeline volume measurements, reconciliation services, and transfer book maintenance for accurate inventory management.

Logistics & Warehousing

Complete storage, stevedoring, customs, and port services with expert import/export consulting for seamless operations.

Stevedoring

Professional cargo handling services including loading, unloading, and securing operations with experienced maritime crews and modern equipment.

Agency

Comprehensive vessel agency services including port clearance, documentation, crew assistance, and coordination with local authorities and suppliers.
